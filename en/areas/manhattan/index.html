FAQ

Manhattan Web Design FAQ

Should Manhattan law firm sites be English-only or bilingual?

Depends on clientele: English-primary with Chinese summaries for US-facing firms; core bilingual pages for Chinatown/community practices. Google indexes both for "Manhattan Chinese lawyer" and related terms. See our Manhattan professional services SEO guide.

How is Manhattan web design different from Flushing or Brooklyn?

Manhattan emphasizes brand polish, speed, clear service boundaries, and compliance (legal disclaimers, healthcare privacy). Keywords focus on Midtown/Downtown/Chinatown + practice area — not neighborhood street names.

Google Ads or SEO first in Manhattan?

High-competition terms (personal injury, med spa) often need Ads for immediate visibility; SEO and content are long-term assets. We recommend website + local SEO baseline, then test Ads on high-intent keywords.

Can we meet in Manhattan?

Yes — Midtown, Downtown, or Chinatown meetings, or video design reviews. First consultation is free.

How long does a Manhattan business website take?

Standard professional sites: 2–4 weeks. Law/clinic sites with multiple practice areas, team pages, or booking: 4–6 weeks with milestones at proposal.
