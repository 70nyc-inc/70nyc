FAQ

Long Island Web Design FAQ

Why must Long Island websites show service areas?

Clients often drive 15–30 minutes; Google ranks partly by distance. Sites and GBP should list towns served (Great Neck, Manhasset, Plainview) to filter unqualified leads. See our Long Island local SEO guide.

How do Long Island customers search on Google?

English: Long Island kitchen remodel, Great Neck contractor, Nassau County dentist. Chinese: 长岛 装修, Great Neck 美容. Landing pages need town + service — not generic "Long Island" alone.

Do Long Island businesses need Google Maps?

Yes. Whether storefront or service-area business, GBP drives the local pack. Upload project photos, service lists, and reviews mentioning job type.

Can we meet on Long Island?

Yes — North Shore and Nassau meetups, or remote collaboration. We plan around seasonal peaks like spring remodeling.

How does Long Island web design pricing compare to Manhattan?

Scope drives price, not geography. Portfolio contractor sites cost more than single-page brochures — proposals list pages, bilingual scope, SEO, and maintenance before sign-off.
