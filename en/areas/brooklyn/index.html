FAQ

Brooklyn Web Design FAQ

How long does a Brooklyn web design project take?

Standard business sites typically launch in 2–4 weeks: discovery, design sign-off, build, and QA. Contractor or restaurant sites with bilingual menus or booking may take longer — we provide a phased timeline at proposal. Sunset Park and Bensonhurst clients can meet locally to speed approvals.

For Sunset Park restaurants, start with a website or Google Maps?

Both — but prioritize claiming and optimizing Google Business Profile while launching a mobile-friendly site with menu pages. Maps drives near-me traffic; your site converts trust into calls. See our Brooklyn local SEO guide.

How is Brooklyn web design different from Manhattan templates?

Brooklyn wins on neighborhood long-tail keywords (Sunset Park contractor, Bensonhurst renovation) rather than skyline-brand terms. We build area-specific landing copy and label portfolio photos by community — not recycled Manhattan layouts.

Can a Chinese-only site work for Brooklyn customers?

Yes for some audiences, but we recommend English summaries on core pages (home, services, contact). Many Brooklyn customers, landlords, and vendors search in English — bilingual sites expand reach and improve Google coverage.

Can we meet in Brooklyn with 70NYC?

Yes — we schedule meetings in Brooklyn, Queens, and Manhattan, or work remotely via video/WeChat. First consultation is free; we recommend a web + SEO + ads stack based on your neighborhood and industry.
