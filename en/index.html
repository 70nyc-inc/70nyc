Project FAQ

Questions About
Working With Us

Common questions from kickoff to delivery — to help you decide faster.

Still have questions? 386-316-1848 Send Inquiry →
StartWhat do I need before we begin?

A business overview, reference websites, existing logo/images, and core features you want. We'll draft a requirements doc after the first call and help fill any gaps — no need to have everything ready upfront.

TimelineHow long does a typical website take?

Standard business sites: 2–4 weeks — week 1 discovery & design, weeks 2–3 development, week 4 testing & launch. E-commerce, booking, or multilingual projects get a detailed timeline during planning.

PricingHow is project cost calculated?

Based on page count, feature complexity, and whether SEO/ads/social are included. After requirements are confirmed, you receive an itemized quote with deliverables per phase — no hidden fees, price locked before signing.

RevisionsCan I change things after design approval?

Reasonable revisions are supported during design approval; major structural changes are completed before development. Minor copy and image swaps during build are fine; out-of-scope features are quoted before work begins.

DeliveryWhat do I receive at launch?

Live website, admin credentials, source code (per contract), training, and SEO setup documentation. Ad accounts and social profiles are handed off with instructions when included.

SupportDo you offer maintenance after launch?

Yes. All projects include post-launch support, with optional annual maintenance — security updates, content changes, monitoring, and incident response. Monthly SEO and ad management are also available.

RemoteCan we work together if I'm not in NYC?

Absolutely. We're headquartered in New York with in-person meetings in Greater NYC. US and international clients collaborate via video, WeChat/WhatsApp, and project tools — same process as local clients.
