What's Included in Our App Development Service

01

Discovery & Prototyping

Map workflows, user roles, and core features into a PRD and interactive prototype. Confirm direction before code — critical for budget-conscious NYC SMBs who can't afford late-stage pivots.

02

UI/UX Design

Follow iOS Human Interface Guidelines and Material Design with clear information architecture and visual identity. Bilingual EN / 中文 interfaces, responsive layouts, and accessibility in mind.

03

Native & Cross-Platform Build

Swift/Kotlin native or React Native cross-platform depending on complexity. Backend APIs, payments, push notifications, and third-party integrations — connected to your existing website or CRM.

04

QA, Launch & Maintenance

Functional testing, performance tuning, App Store and Google Play assets plus review support. Post-launch updates, bug fixes, and feature iterations available.
