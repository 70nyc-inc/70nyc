What's Included in Our Google Ads Service

01

Search Campaigns

Target high-intent keywords like "NYC contractor" or "Flushing salon" with text ads that appear when customers actively search. We optimize bids, match types, and negative keywords to reduce wasted spend.

02

Local & Maps Ads

Combine Google Business Profile with local service ads for "near me" searches. For businesses with a defined service radius in Flushing or Brooklyn, local campaigns often outperform broad traffic.

03

Landing Pages & Tracking

Clicks only matter if they convert. We work with our web design team on forms, call buttons, and page speed, plus GA4 events and call tracking so results are measurable.

04

Transparent Reporting

Weekly or monthly reports on spend, clicks, leads, and ROAS. Budget shifts with seasonality, competition, and business goals — ads are a continuous test-and-learn process, not a set-and-forget switch.
